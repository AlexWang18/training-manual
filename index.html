Intro

Bike commuters should learn basic tune up principles to keep their ride in top shape. In this manual, Pitt students will not only learn the fundamental skills needed for common bike issues, but also gather a range of resources for anything bike related.



Bike issues span far and wide. However by the end of this manual, readers will have the knowledge on hand to fix most problems that could arise. It'll also help readers save money as they will now be equipped with the knowledge of making repairs on their bike. As a result, they won't have to pay for expensive labor at a shop. Just like a car, a bike needs to be maintained, this manual will prepare the reader with the skills to perfrom routine maintenance.
